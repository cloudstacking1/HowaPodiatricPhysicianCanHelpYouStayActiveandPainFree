Understanding the Podiatric Physician

A podiatric physician, also known as a Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM), is a medical professional specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of foot, ankle, and related lower extremity disorders. Their extensive training includes four years of podiatric medical school followed by a residency program, equipping them with the skills to manage a variety of conditions ranging from common ailments to complex surgical cases. ?
